Raidboss Orb



Raidboss Orb is a bullet hell and resource manager, with the ultimate goal of unlocking new characters, new features, and having fun. After choosing a character and entering a game, houses generate and populate the bottom portion of the screen. After some time, a randomly chosen boss will land menacingly onto the playing field, then begin their assault on the village.

The player could be considered the 'raidboss' of the village, and it is their job to fend off the incoming assault on their own, but with the use of resources in the area, and their skills. After the player has survived a certain amount of time, the enemies will retreat, the difficulty will increase, and a new enemy will approach the village once more. This loops infinitely, but provides more coins over time.

Coins are the fundamental motivator for playing the game, allowing players to purchase characters and features to equip or toggle. Features change or add new things to the game that can either help or hinder the player, but all in the goal of having fun, such as weather conditions or terrain hazards.

The first character (currently known as Default), is designed to ease players into the core mechanics of the game. All the abilities are simple and lean into things that all characters try to utilize, such as their own resource to use, mobility, defense, an ultimate for damage, and a healing move.
Zealant (the second character), spreads propaganda throughout the town to convert the villagers to worship them. Utilizing converted homes (Holy Homes), Zealant manifests holy light to fend off enemies.
The third character whom is not currently in the game, is the Mechanic. They will defend and upgrade the village into a mechanical fortress.

The only boss currently in the game is Grimkaldte, General of Hell's Army. They use demonic magic to conjure minions and high-pressure attacks.



Raidboss Orb is my first project post-college that I've worked on, and I plan on working on it consistantly until I can post it on steam someday, although my more achievable goal right now is aiming to put a fun build on itch.io soon. Zealant only has 2 moves, there is only one boss, there are no purchaseable extra features, the core content is still in development at a rapid pace.
