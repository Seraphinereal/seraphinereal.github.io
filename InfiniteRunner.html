Infinite Runner

Game Synopsis

Infinite Runner is a sidescrolling Unity game. Cursed to run indefinitely in a procedurally generated map, dodge lasers and pick up extra health along the way to make it as far as possible. Running out of health or falling out of the world will lead to the timeloop restarting.
Projectiles come from the right side of the screen, each having a small chance to be an extra health instead. The player never stops running, but their speed can be controlled to avoid obstacles.
