Race In A Volcano

-=- -=Game Synopsis=- -=-

After selecting the colour of your car, you enter a race with an NPC in a lava filled volcano. Race to finish two laps first, or else you will be second. Ram your opponents car strategically to send them plummeting into lava, or press the ominous big red button to open up a shortcut for yourself. However, no matter what you do, the volcano track is your forever home until you fall into the lava.
