Arcane Space War

Game Synopsis

Playing as a wizard in a spaceship, the goal is to fight off waves of goblins (also in spaceships with lasers!) while avoiding or mining asteroids that fly across the screen.

The wizard can cast ice or fire spells, toggling with a keybind. Ice spells are hard to land, but will instantly freeze anything they touch. Fire spells are quick and easy, but do much more against frozen enemies and asteroids. Aim for a highscore by surviving and attacking as much as possible.

Experience

Arcane Space War is my second solo game in Unity, I made it start to finish in a week and a half, I worked tirelessly all day everyday because I was determined to have enough spare time to implement a talent card system and new obtainable spells. Unfortunately We were told we had possibly just 5 days to update our portfolios and resumes for field placement applications, so I had to stop development early and switch my priorities to write this right now. If I had the time to, I was going to add a gravity spell that changed the trajectory of asteroids to hit enemy ships as an obtainable talent card.

Everyone in my original group from working on Arcane Undercroft and Fell Gob has made several games revolving around the lineages and history of these two characters, so since I created the characters I thought it was about time for me to make my own continuation. I had a lot of fun working on this project, I'm happy enough with it to look into how to put it on itch.io as my first public project, when I have the spare time to do that of course.
