Golden Wheels

-=- -=Game Synopsis=- -=-

After customizing your car and pressing [START], you start a race against an NPC opponent. If you slip up and fall onto the grass, you lose. If you make two full laps around the map, you win. Collecting 10 coins will activate the Golden Wheels ability, increasing your speed and control drastically. If you win or lose, the camera moves to a view of the finish line with the placements shown.
(The game isn't laggy, the gif I made is capped at 12fps due to its length).
