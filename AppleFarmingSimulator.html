Apple Farming Simulator

-=- -=Game Synopsis=- -=-

You play as a chemist and apple farmer, seeking to grow the Ultimate Apple. Slowly work your way up from a single weak apple tree to an efficient orchard by growing and selling apples, then purchasing upgrades at the workbench. The Ultimate Apple may be 1/100000, but with enough genetic engineering you'll be making them in no time. Upgrade your orchard until you reach the pinnacle of apple production.
