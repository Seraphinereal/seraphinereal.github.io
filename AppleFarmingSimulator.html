Apple Farming Simulator

Game Synopsis

Apple Farming Simulator is a solo project made in Unreal. Playing as a chemist/apple farmer seeking to grow the Ultimate Apple, incrementally upgrade one measely apple tree into a genetically mutated orchard. Grow and sell apples, then purchase upgrades at the workbench. The Ultimate Apple may have a 1/100000 chance of growing, but genetic engineering can solve that issue no sweat. Upgrade the orchard until it reaches the pinnacle of apple production.



Experience

This project was really fun, and my final college-assigned project. I was looking forward to trying my hand at a classic incremental simulator format for a while now. This project took me 3 weeks, and now I can focus on being an intern until I graduate in a few months!
