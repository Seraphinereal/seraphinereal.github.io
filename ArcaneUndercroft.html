Arcane Undercroft

Game Synopsis

Playing as the Basement Wizard locked in a puzzle filled dungeon basement, solve puzzles by using four spells interchangeably. Fire burns wood and rope, magic missile follows the cursor, force launches objects that the player cant push, and ice freezes water. Make it to the cake at the end of the game to win. The win screen depicts Gobby (The Fell Gob), now reigning as a powerful king, battling the now freed Basement Wizard.



Experience
